FAQ

Common Questions from Parents

Does my child need any prior experience?

Absolutely no prior experience is needed. All classes begin from white belt, and our instructors are skilled at welcoming and developing students at every level. We offer a free 2-week trial so you can experience it yourself before committing.

What should we wear to the first class?

For your trial class, comfortable athletic wear is fine. Once enrolled, students wear a traditional Taekwondo uniform (dobok) which can be obtained through the academy.

How often should students attend?

We recommend attending at least twice per week for optimal progress, though students are welcome to attend as many sessions as their schedule allows. Regular attendance accelerates belt progression and skill development.

How long does it take to earn a Black Belt?

The timeline varies by student, age, and training frequency. On average, dedicated students working toward Black Belt should expect several years of consistent training. The journey itself builds tremendous character, discipline, and physical ability along the way.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer free 2-week trial classes so prospective students and parents can experience our academy firsthand before making any commitment. Contact us to get started.
