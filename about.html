About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Our Taekwondo Family

Chung's Taekwondo has served Richmond Hill for over 17 years. Our members range from little kids to grandparents, all training under one roof.

Meet the Head Master

Master Chung

Born in Daegu, South Korea into a lineage of Taekwondo Masters, Master Chung began training in martial arts during his earliest childhood years. His dedication and heritage gave him a depth of knowledge that very few instructors in the world can claim.

Today, he continues to train and develop his skills across multiple disciplines including Taekwondo, Kendo, and Boxing, bringing that breadth of experience into every class he leads at the Richmond Hill academy.

7th Dan Black Belt
WTF Certified Instructor
Physical Education • Ministry of Sports, Korea
20+ Years Teaching Experience
Our Affiliations

World-Recognized Standards

Chung's Taekwondo Academy maintains active membership in both Taekwondo Ontario and the World Taekwondo Federation. These affiliations reflect our commitment to upholding the highest international standards of training, safety, and belt certification.

All Black Belt certificates are issued through Kukkiwon in South Korea, the world governing body for Taekwondo. A Black Belt earned here is recognized around the world.

All instructors hold 4th Degree Black Belt or higher
Kukkiwon-certified Black Belt issuing authority
Member of Taekwondo Ontario
Affiliated with the World Taekwondo Federation
