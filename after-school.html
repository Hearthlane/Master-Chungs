After School Program

Convenient & Comprehensive

After School Program With Pick-up Shuttle from School

We understand how busy life gets for parents. That's why our After School Program includes a shuttle pick-up service from most Richmond Hill public, Catholic, and private elementary schools — taking one more thing off your plate.

Once at the academy, your child will receive the same quality Taekwondo instruction as our evening classes, with the added bonus of homework time and supervised free play before you pick them up. It's the perfect all-in-one after school solution.

How It Works

A Seamless After School Routine

01

School Pick-Up

Our branded, commercially-insured vehicles arrive at your child's school after the school day ends. We serve most Richmond Hill public, Catholic, and private elementary schools.

02

Text Confirmation

You'll receive a text message confirmation the moment your child arrives safely at our academy — giving you complete peace of mind.

03

Taekwondo Class

Students participate in full Taekwondo instruction matching the same curriculum as our evening classes, including belt advancement opportunities.

04

Homework & Free Play

After class, students have access to a dedicated homework study room and supervised free play time until parents arrive for pick-up.

Flexible Scheduling

Enrolment Options to Fit Your Schedule

We offer flexible enrolment from 2 to 5 days a week. After School members also gain access to all evening and weekend classes at no additional charge.

2 Days / Week

Perfect for families with packed schedules. Two days per week still provides meaningful Taekwondo development and a consistent after school routine.

3–4 Days / Week

The most popular option. Balances Taekwondo progress with other extracurricular activities, giving students regular training without overcommitting.

5 Days / Week

For the dedicated martial artist. Full week enrolment maximizes Taekwondo progression and gives parents the most comprehensive childcare coverage.

Not sure which option is right for your family? We're happy to help you choose.
