After School Program

Convenient & Comprehensive

We Pick Them Up. They Come Home Happy.

We know how busy life gets for parents. That's why our After School Program includes shuttle pick-up from most Richmond Hill public, Catholic and private elementary schools. One less thing on your plate.

Once at the academy, your child receives the same quality Taekwondo instruction as our evening classes, with the added bonus of homework time and supervised free play before you pick them up. It's the perfect all-in-one after school solution.

How It Works

How the Afternoon Works

01

School Pick-Up

Our branded, commercially-insured vehicles arrive at your child's school after the school day ends. We serve most Richmond Hill public, Catholic, and private elementary schools.

02

Text Confirmation

You'll get a text confirmation as soon as your child arrives safely at the academy.

03

Taekwondo Class

Students participate in full Taekwondo instruction matching the same curriculum as our evening classes, including belt advancement opportunities.

04

Homework & Free Play

After class, students have access to a dedicated homework study room and supervised free play time until parents arrive for pick-up.

Flexible Scheduling

Enrolment Options to Fit Your Schedule

Choose 2 to 5 days a week. After School members also gain access to all evening and weekend classes at no additional charge.

2 Days / Week

Perfect for families with packed schedules. Two days per week still provides meaningful Taekwondo development and a consistent after school routine.

3–4 Days / Week

The most popular option. Balances Taekwondo progress with other extracurricular activities, giving students regular training without overcommitting.

5 Days / Week

For the dedicated martial artist. Full week enrolment maximizes Taekwondo progression and gives parents the most comprehensive coverage.

Not sure which option is right for your family? We're happy to help you choose.
