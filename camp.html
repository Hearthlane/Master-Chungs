Adventures Await

Exciting Field Trip Destinations

Camp isn't just about training — it's about creating memories. Our camps include exciting off-site excursions that add an adventurous dimension to every week.

Toronto Zoo

One of the largest zoos in the world, the Toronto Zoo gives campers an unforgettable day exploring animals from around the globe.

Tree Top Trekking

An aerial adventure park experience that builds teamwork, bravery, and a love of the outdoors through treetop obstacle courses.

Brooks Farm

A hands-on farm experience where kids connect with nature, learn about agriculture, and enjoy seasonal activities outdoors.
