Welcome to Our Academy

Building Happy, Confident Kids Since 2009

At Chung's Taekwondo Richmond Hill, we help children grow stronger, braver, and more focused — one belt at a time. Located at 9625 Yonge Street, our academy has been a cornerstone of the community for over 17 years, welcoming kids, teens, and adults of every skill level.

Every class blends real martial arts instruction with games, encouragement, and celebration — because kids learn best when they're having fun. Come experience the difference, where every student is family and every belt earned is a milestone celebrated together.
