Welcome to Our Academy

Welcome to Our Taekwondo Family

At Chung's Taekwondo Richmond Hill, we offer a variety of programs designed to meet the needs of our entire community. Located at 9625 Yonge Street Unit A, our academy provides high-quality martial arts instruction to kids, teens, and adults. With over 17 years of experience, we have become a cornerstone in promoting fitness, discipline, and camaraderie right here in Richmond Hill.

Why Parents Choose Us

The Chung's Difference

We go beyond kicks and punches. We help kids build character, confidence and real friendships.

Certified Masters

Every instructor holds at least a 4th Degree Black Belt with Kukkiwon certification from South Korea.

School Shuttle

Pick-up from most Richmond Hill schools, insured under commercial bus insurance, with check-in confirmation for parents.

Kid-First Coaching

Classes for every age, starting with Tots at age 4. Positive, patient teaching that turns energy into focus.

World-Recognized Belts

Members of Taekwondo Ontario and World Taekwondo. Every Black Belt earned here is recognized around the world.
